Who We Are

We are a dedicated department within the University of Hawaii at Manoa, tasked with the crucial role of documenting and collecting data on the endemic plant species that once thrive across the Hawaiian Islands. Our team is comprised of passionate and committed individuals who deeply appreciate the unique natural environment and rich cultural heritage of Hawaii.

We understand that these endemic species are not only vital to the ecological balance of the islands but also hold significant cultural importance. Through our work, we aim to raise awareness, contribute to conservation efforts, and foster a greater understanding of Hawaii's botanical diversity. Together, we strive to protect and preserve the natural beauty that makes Hawaii so special for future generations.
